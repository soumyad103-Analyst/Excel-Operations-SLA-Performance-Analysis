# Excel-Operations-SLA-Performance-Analysis

# Busines Problem 
Operational Delays and SLA breaches are impacting customer satisfaction and increasing operational costs, but the business lacks visibility into where delays occurs and which processes or teams are responsible.

## Dataset Overview
shipment details, geography, hub details, planned tat and actual tat details, cost and revenue , revenue at risk flag, delay flag, calculated columns.

## KPIs Designed
Total shipments, total revenue at risk, total cost overrun, average planned TAT, Average actual TAT, averate Delay Days, SLA breach %, SLA complaince %

# Analysis Approach
Cleaned dataset and created necessary columns
Created pivot table and charts and connected slicers
Created dashboard

# Key Insights
SLA Complaince is critically low (10.55%). Indicating systemic inefficiency and operational failures.

WIth Highest SLA Breaches(40.3%) and backlog load (2.23L), Regional Hub is performing under congestion.

# Recommendations
Recalibrate SLA commitments based on capacity analysis to meet Planned TAT or Increase SLA to realistic level(3-4 days) | SLA compliance % ↑ Revenue at Risk ↓ | High | Ops Head
Re-route excess load to Central Hub or Add more resources (Vehicles, Manpower) | Delay Days ↓ Backlog Load ↓ Cost Overrun ↓ | High | Ops Head
